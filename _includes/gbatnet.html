The **[GBatNet](https://gbatnet.org) Bat Eco-Interactions Working Group**, initially formed to liberate bat-virus data in April 2020 via [COVID19-TAF - Communities Taking Action](https://cetaf.org/covid19-taf-communities-taking-action), a community-rooted initiative raised jointly by [CETAF](https://cetaf.org) and [DiSSCo](https://www.dissco.eu/), aims to: (1) make bat interaction data digitally accessible, (2) align bat taxonomic names to uncover their meaning, and (3) provide open-access to bat literature. The information on this page does not aim to be a complete overview of activities, but rather a minimalist collection of past accomplishments and ongoing activities.
